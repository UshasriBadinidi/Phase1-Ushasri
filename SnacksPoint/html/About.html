About us

WHO WE ARE

San Idli is a locally owned and operated restaurant in San Diego. We care for your health and make sure to serve you only the freshest food. We specialize in South Indian Vegetarian fast food. You will experience the best Idli, Dosa and Chutney in all of San Diego.

WHY CHOOSE US

Experience classic South Indian food using only freshest ingredients, cooked using traditional recipes. We offer authentic fresh vegetarian food in a really nice quaint environment. Come experience the five senses of taste for your taste buds. Hopefully we success story will help encourage you on the path to a new, healthy you!

Our Special menu

Natukodi Biryani

Natukodi biryani is our special, country chicken marinated with a whole range of masalas and cooked with basmathi rice in our authentic hyderabad style.

Dosa

Lentils and rice ground batter turned into crispy dosa in south Indian style. WE serve with fresh coconut and groundnut chutney.

Healthy Vegan Pizza(Gluten free)

Healthy pizza with gluten free crest, vegan cheese made with cashews and loads of veggies like tomato, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, corn.

Shrimp Chow Mein

Boiled noodles stir fried with shrimps, sauces, pepper, salt, garlic and spring onions
